# Project Instructions

These instructions apply to all bathroom-remodel planning and documentation in this repository.

## Planning Conflicts

Always compare new information against existing documented decisions, directions, assumptions, and open questions.

When a conflict or meaningful ambiguity appears:

1. Call it out explicitly to Patrick and Melissa.
2. Explain which statements or decisions conflict.
3. Do not silently choose one option or overwrite the earlier information.
4. Mark the affected decision as open or conflicting in the appropriate project file.
5. Preserve the useful reasoning behind each option.
6. Ask Patrick and Melissa for clarity before treating the decision as settled.
7. Ask no more than three clarification questions at a time.

A conflict includes:

- Two selections that cannot both be implemented
- A new preference that contradicts an earlier confirmed decision
- A proposed product or method that is incompatible with another selection
- A budget, schedule, layout, or scope assumption that no longer fits the plan
- Language that could reasonably be interpreted in more than one materially different way

## Decision Classification

Keep these categories distinct:

- **Confirmed decision:** explicitly approved and ready to guide the project
- **Current direction:** preferred approach that still needs final details
- **Option under consideration:** possible approach that has not been selected
- **Reference estimate:** preliminary pricing or guidance that must be verified
- **Open question:** missing information needed before finalizing a decision
- **Conflict:** incompatible or contradictory planning information requiring clarification

Do not promote a direction, option, estimate, or assumption into a confirmed decision without clear approval.

## Clarification Workflow

- Ask questions in groups of no more than three.
- Document each answer in the appropriate decision vertical.
- Continue asking questions until the active vertical has enough clarity to satisfy its MVP exit criteria.
- When a conflict blocks the active vertical, prioritize resolving it in the next question group.

## Decision Dumps

When processing an unstructured project dump, follow [DUMP.md](DUMP.md) in addition to these instructions.
