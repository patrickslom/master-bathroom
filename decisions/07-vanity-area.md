# Vanity Area

## Outcome

Finalize a functional vanity package and decide whether to retain or replace the existing black granite countertop.

## Decisions Required

- Can the existing countertop and cabinets remain without repair or modification?
- Which vessel sinks fit the existing cutouts and available counter depth?
- Which faucets fit the sinks and hole locations?
- Will cabinets be repainted, and if so, what color?
- What mirror sizes and shapes fit the wall and lighting plan?
- What hardware and accessories coordinate with the selected metal finish?

## Current Direction

- Compare retaining the existing black granite against replacing it with a quartz or granite remnant
- Quartz is the preferred replacement material for low maintenance and moisture resistance
- Look for quartz or granite remnants as a cost-saving replacement option
- Use white, non-round vessel sinks
- Add new faucets, mirrors, lighting, and hardware
- Consider repainting the cabinets
- Use a clean painted wall behind the vanity or a subtle timeless accent

## Countertop Reference Estimates

These are rough planning estimates from the decision dump, not committed budget amounts. Verify using actual local quotes.

| Option | Rough Estimate | Included / Additional Considerations |
|---|---:|---|
| Installed granite, approximately 8 feet | $1,500-$3,000 | Varies by stone, edge, cutouts, and labor |
| Quartz, approximately 8 feet | $1,000-$2,000+ | Rough material-level comparison; verify fabrication and installation |
| Quartz or granite remnant | $500-$1,200 plus fabrication | Availability and usable dimensions may limit selection |

Fabrication may add sink cutouts, edge finishing, faucet holes, and installation labor.

## Recommended MVP

Price retaining the countertop and replacing it with a suitable remnant before committing. Retain the existing cabinets, select compatible sinks and faucets after the countertop decision, and defer cabinet repainting if necessary. Confirm mirror and lighting dimensions together before purchasing either.

## Dependencies

- Accurate vanity and countertop measurements
- Plumbing fixture finish
- Electrical and lighting plan
- Overall finish package

## MVP Exit Criteria

- [ ] Countertop and cabinet condition approved
- [ ] Sink and faucet fit confirmed
- [ ] Mirror and lighting dimensions coordinated
- [ ] Cabinet paint decision made or explicitly deferred
- [ ] Hardware and accessory finish selected
