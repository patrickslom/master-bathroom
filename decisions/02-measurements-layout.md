# Measurements and Layout

## Outcome

Create an accurate existing-condition record and confirm the final layout before products or rough-in changes are committed.

## Decisions Required

- Does the shower retain its existing footprint?
- Will the shower entry be curbed or curbless?
- Will the existing bench be removed without replacement?
- Where will shower storage be located?
- Will the existing drain remain or move?
- Do door swing, window, toilet, vanity, and shower clearances work?
- Are any walls being removed, moved, or opened permanently?

## Measurements Required

- Full room dimensions and ceiling height
- Shower footprint, wall heights, curb, entry, and drain location
- Wall thicknesses and openings
- Vanity, countertop, sink cutouts, and faucet holes
- Toilet centerline and clearances
- Door and window dimensions
- Existing plumbing, electrical, lighting, and ventilation locations
- Main bathroom floor area and shower tile areas

## Recommended MVP

Retain the existing fixture locations and shower footprint unless doing so prevents correct drainage, waterproofing, access, or the desired bench removal.

## Dependencies

- Scope and budget
- Post-demolition findings may require revision

## MVP Exit Criteria

- [ ] Dimensioned existing-layout sketch completed
- [ ] Final intended layout documented
- [ ] Shower footprint, entry, bench, storage, and drain plan confirmed
- [ ] Product-critical measurements independently checked twice
- [ ] Layout reviewed by the people performing framing, plumbing, and shower work
